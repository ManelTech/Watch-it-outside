About us

Watch it outside Boston edition is an outdoor film festival organized by Watch it outside events & Co. Our organizer and the founder of Watch it outside events & Co Clara Dunn hopes to select and show films in Boston Public Garden from August 5th to 8th. With a fine collection of movies that can appeal to audiences of all ages,she invites you all to enjoy your summer evenings from 6 pm till midnight.The festival is free and open for everyone. We can't wait to see you!
